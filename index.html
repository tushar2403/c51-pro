Cube

  • 1.Fold your paper in half from left to right and unfold. You now have a central crease.
  • 2.Fold the left and right edges to the central crease and unfold.
  • 3.Fold the top left corner and bottom right corner inwards, aligning with the two previous creases.
  • 4.Fold the lower-left section diagonally up to the right along the diagonal flap that is in the right section.
  • 5.Tuck the flap you just created underneath the right section.
  • 6.Rotate the paper and fold the lower-left corner up in the same way as before.
  • 7.Insert the flap underneath the lower right section.
  • 8.Flip the model over to the other side, left to right.
